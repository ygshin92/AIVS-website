Research

Our research focuses on computer vision, multimodal AI, and embodied intelligence. This page highlights our main directions and representative topics.

Generative AI

Diffusion models, controllable generation, efficient inference, and safety.

Vision & Multimodal Learning

Representation learning, VLMs, fine-grained recognition, and robust perception.

Embodied AI & Robotics

Perception-action loops, manipulation, planning, and data-efficient learning.

Selected Keywords

Diffusion, Controllable Generation, Linear Attention, Vision-Language Models, Segmentation, Adversarial Robustness, Embodied Agents, Manipulation, Data-Efficient Learning

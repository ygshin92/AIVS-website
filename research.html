Research

Our research focuses on computer vision, multimodal AI, and embodied intelligence. This page highlights our main directions and representative topics.

Generative AI

We study modern generative models including diffusion models and GAN variants, with an emphasis on controllable generation, efficient inference, and safety. Our goal is to build practical generation pipelines that can be deployed reliably under real-world constraints.

Diffusion Controllable Generation Efficiency Safety

Vision & Multimodal Learning

We develop robust visual representations and multimodal learning methods, including vision-language models (VLMs), fine-grained recognition, and reliable perception. We also explore training strategies that improve generalization and robustness in diverse environments.

VLM Representation Learning Segmentation Robust Perception

Embodied AI & Robotics

Our embodied AI research connects perception to action through planning and control. We investigate data-efficient learning for manipulation and interactive decision-making, aiming to build embodied agents that operate reliably in the physical world.

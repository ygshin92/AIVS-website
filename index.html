AI Vision & Systems (AIVS) Laboratory

At AIVS Lab, we build real-world intelligent systems that perceive, reason, and act. Our research spans computer vision, multimodal foundation models, and embodied intelligence, with a focus on robust, scalable, and deployable AI.

Research Pillars

Generative AI

We study modern generative models including diffusion models, GAN variants, and recent video and 3D/4D generative frameworks, with an emphasis on controllability, efficiency, and safe deployment.

Diffusion Models Video Generation 3D / 4D Generation Controllable Generation

Vision & Multimodal Learning

We develop robust visual representations and multimodal learning methods that combine visual and language signals, focusing on reliable perception, generalization under shift, and trustworthy model behavior in the wild.

Vision-Language Models Multimodal Representation Fine-Grained Recognition Trustworthy Perception

Embodied AI & Robotics

Our embodied AI research bridges perception and action through planning, control, and interactive decision-making. We study data-efficient methods for manipulation and aim to build embodied agents that operate reliably in complex physical environments.

Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Embodied Agents Robotic Manipulation Planning & Control

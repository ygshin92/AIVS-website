AIVS Lab, Korea University

Who We Are

We study intelligent systems that connect perception, reasoning, and action. Our research spans computer vision, multimodal foundation models, and embodied intelligence. We focus on building robust, scalable, and deployable AI systems that operate reliably in real-world environments.
